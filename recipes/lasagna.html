Description

This is a delicious recipe that combines the flavors of ingredients. "Kottu Roti," a popular Sri Lankan street food dish. Kottu Roti is made with chopped flatbread, vegetables, eggs, and a blend of spices. Here's a basic list of ingredients commonly used in making Kottu Roti

Ingredients

  • 4-6 pieces of Roti or Paratha, shredded or chopped
  • 1 medium-sized carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 leek or spring onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, thinly sliced
  • Optional: 1/2 cup beans or other vegetables of your choice
  • 2 eggs, beaten (or cooked chicken, beef, or mutton)
  • Optional: 1/2 cup cooked and shredded chicken or meat of your choice
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • A handful of curry leaves (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 2-3 tablespoons oil or butter for stir-frying

Steps

  1. Prepare Roti: Shred or chop the roti into small pieces.
  2. Prep Vegetables and Protein: Julienned carrots, thinly slice bell pepper, onion, leek, and cabbage. Beat eggs.
  3. Stir-fry Vegetables and Protein: In a large pan, stir-fry vegetables, protein, and shredded roti together.
  4. Add Spices and Sauce: Add curry powder, turmeric powder, chili powder, curry leaves, salt, pepper, and soy sauce. Mix well.
  5. Continue Stir-frying: Keep stirring and chopping with spatulas until everything is well-mixed and cooked.
  6. Serve Hot: Once cooked, serve the Kottu Roti hot.
